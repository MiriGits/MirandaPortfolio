Technical writer by day,
fiction writer by night.

My name is Miranda De la Victoria and I grew up in rural Wisconsin, chasing stories through the woods.

No matter the content, I love the challenge of writing and editing. I’m a quick learner with endless curiosity and the desire to impact people’s lives.

I'm currently pursuing a master's degree in scientific and technical communication from the University of Minnesota. I have a bachelor's degree in English from the University of Texas at San Antonio and I attended the Columbia Publishing Course, where I learned all aspects of book publishing.

During the weekday, I'm a technical writer intern at 3M. When I'm not at work, I can be found enjoying many things: eating ramen, wandering the woods, writing a novel, drawing, reading, and learning to code (HTML, CSS, XML, and DITA).
